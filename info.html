Managing E-Waste at LPU

At Lovely Professional University, we are committed to reducing e-waste by encouraging the recycling and proper disposal of electronic devices.

How to Recycle E-Waste on Campus:

  • Drop your old devices at the designated e-waste bins across campus.
  • Join our e-waste awareness programs and workshops.
  • Use refurbished devices instead of buying new ones.

Facts About E-Waste:

  • 80% of e-waste is improperly disposed of globally.
  • Hazardous materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium are present in e-waste.
  • Recycling e-waste saves energy and reduces pollution.
