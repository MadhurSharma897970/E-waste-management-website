Join us in making Lovely Professional University a green campus by managing electronic waste effectively.

What is E-Waste?

Electronic waste, or e-waste, refers to discarded electronic devices and components that pose a threat to the environment.

Why is E-Waste a Problem?

E-waste contains hazardous materials, and improper disposal leads to pollution and health risks. It's essential to manage and recycle e-waste properly.
